Design Achievements:

This project successfully designed and implemented a prototype Water Monitoring System for Farms, demonstrating the potential of Internet of Things (IoT) technology to improve farm management practices. Here are the key achievements:

Real-Time Water Level Monitoring: The system utilizes a water level sensor to provide continuous monitoring of water availability in troughs, ensuring a consistent supply of fresh water for animals.

Automated Refill System: By integrating a pump and a control unit, the system automates the refilling process, eliminating the need for manual intervention and saving valuable time and resources.

Animal Presence Detection : The design incorporates an ultrasonic sensor for animal presence detection near the water trough and sends a signal to the servo motor for salt licks dispension. This functionality protects the salt licks from bad weather since it's only dispensed when there are animals available.
